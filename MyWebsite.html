// 03. project

Featured Project

MECHANICAL ROBOTICS · DEC 2025
DC Motor Driven Walking Robot

A minimalist mechanical walking robot built entirely from a DC motor, a battery, and chopsticks. No microcontroller. No code. Just pure crank-linkage mechanism design inspired by Theo Jansen's Strandbeest. The robot converts rotational motor motion into a walking gait through carefully phased leg linkages — proving that complex locomotion is fundamentally a mechanical problem.
